Definition of HTML Attributes

What They Are: Attributes are special characteristics of HTML elements that are defined in the opening tag. They typically come in name/value pairs, separated by an equal sign.

Key Characteristics

Syntax: Attributes are written as part of an HTML tag. The general syntax is attribute="value".
Purpose: Attributes modify or provide information about an element, such as its appearance, behavior, or other settings.
Common Attributes: Many HTML elements share common attributes.
